QNX Software Systems

May 2011 to March 2012

At QNX I worked as a programmer on the graphics team where I worked to optimize the BlackBerry home screen for both the Playbook and Z10 as well as working on some home screen features such as folders, effects and the status bar.

It was on this team that I learned to work in C, on embedded systems and with OpenGL:ES.
